Our process

Crafted the way it was always meant to be done

We source only the top 5% of hides from Italian and Spanish tanneries. Each piece passes through the hands of a single craftsperson — no assembly lines, no shortcuts.

Wax-thread hand stitching, not machine-sewn
Free resoling and conditioning for life

Natural tanning

Hand cutting
